    Car Maintenance Guides

    Regular Maintenance Schedule

    • Every 3,000-5,000 miles: Oil and filter change
    • Every 5,000-8,000 miles: Tire rotation
    • Every 15,000-30,000 miles: Air filter replacement
    • Every 30,000 miles: Transmission fluid change
    • Every 50,000 miles: Battery replacement

    Basic Maintenance Tips

    • Check tire pressure monthly
    • Inspect brakes every 6 months
    • Check all fluid levels monthly
    • Listen for unusual noises
    • Keep your car clean inside and out

    Car Repair Guides

    Car Repair Guides

    Engine Repairs

    Easy

    Steps:

    1. Warm up engine (2-3 minutes)
    2. Jack up car safely and secure with jack stands
    3. Locate drain plug and oil filter
    4. Drain old oil
    5. Replace oil filter
    6. Add new oil
    7. Check oil level
    Warning: Ensure engine is not too hot before starting work.
    Medium

    Steps:

    1. Let engine cool completely
    2. Locate and clean spark plug area
    3. Remove spark plug wire/coil
    4. Remove old spark plug
    5. Gap new spark plug
    6. Install new spark plug
    7. Reattach wire/coil
    Warning: Do not overtighten spark plugs.

    Brake System

    Medium

    Steps:

    1. Loosen lug nuts before jacking
    2. Jack up car and secure with stands
    3. Remove wheel
    4. Remove caliper bolts
    5. Remove old pads
    6. Install new pads
    7. Reinstall caliper
    8. Reinstall wheel
    Warning: Always test brakes before driving at high speeds.

    Electrical System

    Easy

    Steps:

    1. Identify battery location
    2. Disconnect negative terminal first
    3. Disconnect positive terminal
    4. Remove battery hold-down clamp
    5. Remove old battery
    6. Install new battery
    7. Connect positive terminal first
    8. Connect negative terminal
    Warning: Never connect terminals in reverse order.
